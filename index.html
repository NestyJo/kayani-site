About Us

Welcome to Kayani African Safari

Kayani African Safari is a leading eco-conscious safari company based in Tanzania, founded by Andrew Kayani in 2019. Dedicated to blending adventure with sustainability, the company offers tailored safari experiences that showcase the beauty of Tanzania's iconic wildlife and landscapes.

Kayani African Safari emphasizes mental wellness, cultural exchange, and community empowerment by integrating sustainable practices and supporting local initiatives.

Services

Our Services

Guided Safari Tours

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime safari in some of Tanzania’s most famous national parks, including Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater, Tarangire, and more. Our guided safari tours are designed to bring you face-to-face with Africa’s stunning wildlife in their natural habitats.

Wellness and Mental Health Safaris

Our Wellness and Mental Health Safaris are designed to help you unwind, relax, and recharge while immersing yourself in Tanzania’s breathtaking landscapes. We offer safaris that combine adventure with mental wellness, focusing on nature’s healing power.

Cultural and Community Immersion

At Kayani African Safari, we believe in connecting our clients to the heart of local communities. During your safari, you’ll have the opportunity to engage with indigenous cultures, learn about their traditions, and support community-driven initiatives.

Photography Tours

For photography enthusiasts, our Photography Tours provide the perfect opportunity to hone your skills while capturing Tanzania’s unparalleled natural beauty. Our experienced guides and photographers will help you make the most of your safari with tailored photography experiences.

Corporate and Group Tours

Looking to build team spirit or provide your employees with a unique and relaxing experience? Our Corporate and Group Tours are designed to foster teamwork, enhance leadership, and promote well-being.

Hotel Reservation

We understand that choosing the right place to stay is a crucial part of your safari experience, we offer a comprehensive hotel reservation service to ensure that you have comfortable, well-located, and eco-friendly accommodations throughout your journey in Tanzania.

Transport and Logistics Services

We offer all the transportation services you need for a smooth and comfortable safari experience. Whether you’re flying into Tanzania or traveling between parks, we ensure that your journey is as enjoyable as the destination.

Workshops Management

we believe in providing immersive, hands-on experiences that go beyond sightseeing. Our Workshops are designed to give you the opportunity to learn new skills, explore Tanzanian culture, and deepen your understanding of conservation, photography, wellness, and more.
